Course Aims

This course introduces probability theory and statistical inference. It will help students learn the theoretical basis and practical applications of probability distributions, and understand the theory of statistical inference as developed from the basis of probability. It trains students in thinking and analyzing problems from a probabilistic and statistical point of view.
